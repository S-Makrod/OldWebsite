A Little About Me

Hi, I'm Saad! I am a current second year at the University of Toronto. I enjoy coding and building new things, one of the reasons I pursued Computer Science was so that I could build software that everyone can use. I find it really interesting that something I make today can be used by someone else tomorrow, just like this website!

In my free time I enjoy reading and playing ice hockey. I played ice hockey for six years as a goaltender but sadly due to the pandemic it has been a while since I have stepped on the ice. I enjoy my quiet time and love a good work-life balance.

I hope you enjoy looking through the rest of my website!
